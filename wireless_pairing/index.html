    Wireless Pairing

    Step 0: Charge the tracker with a USB cable.Charge a tundra tracker until its LED turns XXX.

    Step 1: Connect a dongle to your PC.A Tundra Tracker can be paired with one dongle connected to your PC. Step 2. Turn the tracker on.Press the power button on top of the tracker until its LED turns blue. Step 3. Set SteamVR into pairing mode.On your PC, start SteamVR and select “Devices” -> “Pair Controller” -> “HTC VIVE Tracker” on its menu. Step 4. Press and hold the power button of the tracker to pair. LED starts blinking in blue when it enters pairing mode. It turns green when it’s paired with a dongle and Tundra Tracker’s icon appears on SteamVR window.
